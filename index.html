The purpose of this site is for r0d to create a repository of quick notes about hacking topics that he learns about over the course of his four years at West Point and potentially beyond.

The intent is to have a simple yet still l33t looking site where he can quickly throw up write-ups for topics and reinforce his learning through teaching, a process that has served him well throughtout the years for other topics.

Hopefully, as r0d's l33t h4x0r skills grow, the depth and variety of this site will grow, serving both to remind r0d of what he has learned and help others follow in his footsteps.
